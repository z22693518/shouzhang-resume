About DJ Shou

With over a decade in the electronic music scene, DJ Shou Zhang has become a prominent figure in Asia's underground techno movement. Known for marathon sets that journey through deep, hypnotic grooves to peak-time techno bangers, Shou's unique sound has captivated dance floors from Tokyo to Berlin.

Starting from underground warehouse parties in Taipei, Shou's relentless passion and innovative mixing style quickly gained recognition, leading to performances at major festivals including Ultra, Tomorrowland, and Amsterdam Dance Event.
